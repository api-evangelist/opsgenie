Introducing Flex: A Flexible Commercial Model for the AI Era Built for enterprise agility, a new value-based model helps customers keep pace with AI-driven change Atlassian will introduce Flex, a new licensing approach to help complex enterprises adopt Atlassian’s AI-powered platform with speed and flexibility amid rapidly transforming business needs. Rather than predict usage years in advance, Atlassian’s largest customers will be able to flex, adopt, and scale across Atlassian’s portfolio — ensuring their Atlassian investment keeps pace with how their teams actually work.
